Our goal:

At Radi-U, we promote interaction between us and you, the listener, unlike any other radio station!
We strive to give the best possible radio experience for our listeners, thus we take your feedback to heart and let your voices be heard.
With our unique platform, we will play music voted on by you, with a chart designed by the votes of our listeners!

The team:

As a group of computing science students, we have come together and combined what we have learnt at university and from previous experience to create Radi-U, the radio for you!

How to use Radi-U:

#1 Register:

Create an account with us in under a minute and choose your favourite song!

#2 Find your music:

Our homepage shows the top songs of the week, but feel free to search for your own music and give it a like! Once you have liked a few songs, our algorithms will suggest songs you might also enjoy.

#3 Listen!

Listen to your favourite music and discover more!
